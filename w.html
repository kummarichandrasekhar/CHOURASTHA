What is special in Bhadrachalam?

Image result for bhadrachalam tourism

Bhadrachalam is a key town of Pilgrimage importance located in the Khammam District of Telangana.


The temple town of Bhadrachalam is situated on the banks of Godavari River.
It was part of East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh till 1959.
The town is synonymous for its famous temple devoted to Lord Rama.
